About WorkR

WorkR helps you keep track of your daily working hours and any extra/overtime you accumulate.

Ideal for freelancers, shift workers, or anyone managing flexible work hours.

Easily log your hours, view your work history, and stay organized.

Built with Ionic and Angular for a fast, modern, mobile-first experience.

Fully functional offline, so you can track hours anywhere, anytime.
